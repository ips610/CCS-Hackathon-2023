Our Mission
"
A billion acts of courage can spark a brighter tomorrow
"
Think Ambiente is a leading environmental NGO in India working towards achieving road cleanliness. We seek to create a platform for global engagement where everyone is doing their bit to combat the solid waste management crisis. We invite people out of their comfort zones to take courageous action with us, individually in their daily lives and in community with others who share our commitment to a better world. A green and peaceful future is our quest. The heroes of our story are all of us who believe that a better world is not only within reach but is being built today.

About Us

In 2021, a group of activist friends came together in Patiala, Punjab with the idea that is taking shape into a powerful movement. Around the globe, we are standing up for our communities and holding governments and corporations accountable. Our Planet - a pale blue dot on the vast canvas of the universe, is fast approaching a point of no return. In the current world order, materialism supersedes human values, the economy is more treasured than ecology, and profits are prioritized over people. This ceaseless demand for more is pushing our soil, air, water, food, forests, and climate closer to an inevitable collapse. Think Ambiente exists because of activists, supporters, donors, and volunteers who believe in taking collective action for the environment. Our Planet needs us more than ever - individuals, communities, and groups who have the power to shift the paradigm.

Waste Management Tips

Waste management is important as it saves the environment from the toxic effects of inorganic and biodegradable element present in waste.Waste can be recycled if collected and managed efficiently.

Segregating Waste

Waste segregation can be defined as the process of identifying, classifying,dividing and sorting of garbage and waste products in an effort to reduce, reuse and recycle materials.
