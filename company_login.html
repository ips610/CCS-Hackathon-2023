We are more than just a company

Our goal is not limited to creation of a particular website, but a movement towards a healthier environment. What makes us, us is the passion for the cause. Therefore, each and every one of you who is willing to do something for our shared environment, to preserve it, to make it safe for our future, is a part of this movement. We invite people out of their comfort zones to take courageous action with us, individually in their daily lives, and in community with others who share our commitment to a better world. Think Ambiente exists because of activists, supporters, donors, and volunteers who believe in taking collective action for the environment. Our Planet needs us more than ever - individuals, communities, and groups who have the power to shift the paradigm.
